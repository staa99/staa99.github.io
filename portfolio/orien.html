Orien

Orien is a really nice strategy game that borrows concepts from tic-tac-toe. It can be played by up to four people. It started out as a fun project to get something on the Play Store but I tried to make money from it and failed.
Now it's fully free: no ads, no in-app purchases, just have fun with friends.
