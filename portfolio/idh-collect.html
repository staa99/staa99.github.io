ODK Collect Extension

ODK Collect is an open source data collection app. I extended it to provide additional features including editing sent forms and generating IDs based on specified parameters.
I extended ODK Collect open source app and added features aid IDH's research in Nigeria since 2018, started with the 2018 Cassava research.
